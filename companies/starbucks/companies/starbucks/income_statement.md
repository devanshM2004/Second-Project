# Starbucks Simplified Income Statement

## Assumptions

* Revenue: $100.00
* Cost of Goods Sold (COGS): 35.00% of revenue
* Operating Expenses (SG&A): 30.00% of revenue
* Depreciation: 5.00% of revenue
* Interest Expense: $3.00
* Tax Rate: 25.00%

## Income Statement

| Line Item          |  Amount |
| ------------------ | ------: |
| Revenue            | $100.00 |
| Cost of Goods Sold |  $35.00 |
| Gross Profit       |  $65.00 |
| Operating Expenses |  $30.00 |
| Depreciation       |   $5.00 |
| Operating Income   |  $30.00 |
| Interest Expense   |   $3.00 |
| Pretax Income      |  $27.00 |
| Taxes              |   $6.75 |
| Net Income         |  $20.25 |

## Notes

This is a simplified educational model designed to show the structure of an income statement and how assumptions flow into profitability.
