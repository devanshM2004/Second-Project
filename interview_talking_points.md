# Starbucks Interview Talking Points

## Project Summary
I built a simplified financial statement case study for Starbucks using AI as a learning and productivity tool. The goal was to better understand how the income statement, balance sheet, and cash flow statement work together and how business assumptions affect financial results.

## What I Actually Did
- defined financial assumptions
- built a simplified income statement
- built a simplified balance sheet
- built a simplified cash flow statement
- documented business insights and statement linkage
- organized the full project in GitHub

## What I Learned
- how revenue flows through costs and expenses into net income
- how the balance sheet reflects financial position
- how cash flow differs from accounting profit
- how the three statements connect at a basic level
- how AI can accelerate learning when used carefully

## Why I Built It
I wanted to go beyond classroom theory and build something practical that reflects initiative, self-learning, and the ability to use AI in a professional workflow.

## How I’d Explain It in an Interview
This project was my way of learning financial statements in a more hands-on way. I used AI to help structure the work, but I focused on understanding the assumptions, how the statements connect, and what the outputs actually mean from a business perspective.

- incorporate real Starbucks financial data
- expand into forecasting and scenario analysis
